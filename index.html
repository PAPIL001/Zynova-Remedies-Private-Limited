About Us

Zynova Remedies is a leading Indian-branded pharmaceutical company and part of the Zynova Group, established in 2012. With expertise in APIs, finished dosage formulations, and third-party manufacturing, we are committed to delivering innovative and affordable healthcare solutions. By integrating AI and machine learning, we are advancing drug discovery, optimizing processes, and improving diabetes management. Partnering with governments and institutions, we ensure essential medicines and vaccines reach those who need them most.
