About Zynova Remedies

Zynova Remedies is an Indian-branded pharmaceutical company and part of the Zynova Group, established in 2012. We have a strong presence in manufacturing Active Pharmaceutical Ingredients (APIs), finished dosage formulations, and third-party manufacturing. Through the use of Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning, we are driving advancements in drug discovery, optimizing production processes, and developing innovative diabetes management solutions.

Zynova Group works closely with state governments and institutions to supply vaccines, medicines, and healthcare essentials. With a focus on quality, affordability, and innovation, we are committed to improving healthcare outcomes across India and beyond.
